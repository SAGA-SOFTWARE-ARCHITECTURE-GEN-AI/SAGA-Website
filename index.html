Research

What We Investigate

Our collaboration spans the full spectrum of software intelligence,from making existing systems legible to AI, to building new systems that embed AI safely and dependably.

Architecture Intelligence

Using large language models to automatically recover, reconstruct, and document software architectures from source code, enabling systems to understand themselves.

LLM-Integrated Systems

Architectural principles, patterns, and design tactics for building software systems that reliably and securely incorporate language model components at scale.

Quality & Security Engineering

Detecting anti-patterns, mitigating adversarial threats such as prompt injection, and verifying the correctness and dependability of AI-powered software systems.

Edge & Constrained Deployment

Optimizing small language models for resource-constrained environments where latency and power are critical constraints.
